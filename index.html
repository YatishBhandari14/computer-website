Yatish Public School

The Yatish Public School , Rajouri Garden was established on 14th June 2010 by Mr. Yatish Bhandari as a Primary School. Over the years, the School has grown in size as well as in strength and had been upgraded to High school in the 13th June 2016. Presently, there are 170 teachers, 71 administrative staff and 104 permanent MTS. It is one of the famous and popular unit run primary schools. The school is well equipped with an air conditioned computer lab, an overhead projector, smart boards, activity room and a LED T.V. The school has a big play ground to facilitate sports and games activities of the school. The school is registered under the society Act with the Yatish public Educational and Cultural Society Vide Registration No. PS/61/AIRHQ/2012.

The school aims at the holistic development of students providing state of the art education. We aim at inculcating values of honesty, integrity, leadership, self confidence, tolerance and spirit of sportsmanship in little minds. The tiny tots are encouraged to develop a bond of humanism with an inquisitive temperament. The school environment provides ample opportunity to imbibe true values of the school motto. Education cultivates the right attitude and teaches the students to be humble and strive to prove that they are the worthy citizens of the Nation. Education, in the Yatish Public environment nurtures the young impressionable minds to mould into healthy blossoms of tomorrow
